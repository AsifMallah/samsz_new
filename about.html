Who We Are

Crafting Memories One Scoop at a Time

Welcome to SamsZ Ice Cream, where we've been crafting traditional ice cream from day one. Our passion is to create the finest, most delicious ice cream that brings joy to every scoop.

We use only the highest quality ingredients, sourced from local farms and trusted suppliers. Our time-honored recipes ensure each bite is rich, creamy, and bursting with authentic flavor. From the classic Kulfa to the bold Conezilla, every product carries the SamsZ promise.

Based in Karachi, Pakistan, we serve families, businesses, and ice cream lovers across the city. Our factory outlet is where quality meets passion — and where every frozen treat begins its journey to your hands.
